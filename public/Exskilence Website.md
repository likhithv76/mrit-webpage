**The Exskilence-MRIT Partnership \- to shape the future of tech talent**

The rapid pace of technological innovation with the advent of Artificial Intelligence (AI) means that a traditional engineering curriculum, while foundational, needs to be supplemented with practical, hands-on training on the latest tools and technologies. 

With this end in view, we plan to work in partnership with colleges, where the colleges take care of the academic performance of the students, while we address their skilling and practical training needs. 

# In this regard, we have partnered with Mysuru Royal Institute of Technology (MRIT), an engineering college in Mandya, Karnataka.MRIT was established in the year 2015\. The founders of the college are all eminent retired professors of engineering colleges. 

# 

# Our partnership with MRIT works on both the tracks \- of 

# (a) academic learning, and 

# (b) skilling / practical training \- progress in parallel for 12 months \- right through the 6th and 7th semester of the engineering studies.

Early outcomes of this collaboration are very promising, with several MRIT students of the 2023-24 and 2024-25 passing out batch finding placements in companies like 

Deloitte, Zeta, Ignite IQ, Thought Process, Socgen and Rythmos, with competitive salary packages.
